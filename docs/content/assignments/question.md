## Objectives

In an ~8-minute-long presentation{{< footnote >}}Minimum length: 7 minutes. Maximum length: 8 minutes and 30 seconds.{{< /footnote >}} your team will:

- Provide some background on members' expertise.
- Introduce us to 1 to 3 topics or questions that you would like to examine using AI/ML. These topics can be very broad (e.g., "we would like to explore volcanic activity") to very specific (e.g., "we are interested in detecting craters on the moon"). 
- Tell us *why these topics are interesting to you.* Then, explain *why they are important to others (e.g., scientists, community members, humanity at large).
- Identify several sources of data that may be of use to your team. Provide some basic metadata. 
- Produce preliminary characterizations or visualizations of the data.
